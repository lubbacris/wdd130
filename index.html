Gauteng

Gauteng lies on the great interior plateau of Southern Africa (the Highveld) and stretches northward from the Vaal River. South of Pretoria there is rolling grassland, and to the north of the city is bushveld. The provinces climate is warm and temperate, and rainfall averages 26 inches (650 mm) annually. Several languages are spoken in the province; the most widely spoken are Zulu, Afrikaans, Sotho, and English.
